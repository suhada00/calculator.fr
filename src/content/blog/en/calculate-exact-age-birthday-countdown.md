Most of us know our age in years. However, finding your exact age in **years, months, and days** is a bit trickier than it looks. This difficulty stems from the Gregorian calendar's irregularities, such as months varying between 28, 30, and 31 days, and the occurrence of leap years.

## The Logic of Exact Age Calculation

To calculate the exact time elapsed between your date of birth and a target date, you must perform chronological subtractions:

1. **Calculate Year Difference:** Subtract the birth year from the target year.
2. **Adjust Months:** If the target month is before the birth month, or if it is the same month but the target day is less than the birth day, we subtract 1 year and add 12 to the month count.
3. **Adjust Days:** If the target day is less than the birth day, we borrow the number of days from the previous month.

## Handling Leap Years

Every four years, a leap year occurs (e.g., 2024, 2028), adding February 29th to the calendar. Any accurate age tracking program must reference the precise number of days in February for each given year to guarantee that the results are not off by a day.

## Counting Days to Your Next Birthday

To find out how many days are left until your next celebration:
* Create a temporary date representing your birthday in the current year.
* If that date has already passed, set the year to the next calendar year.
* Calculate the difference in milliseconds between that target date and today, and convert the result into a clean number of days.
