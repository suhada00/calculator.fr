# Simulateur Plus

> Simulateur Plus is a premium, high-speed, multilingual web platform offering a suite of 19 accurate financial, health, unit conversion, and everyday calculators for European markets (primarily France and English-speaking users).

## Core Calculators

- [/en/calculators/](https://www.simulateurplus.com/en/calculators/) - Dynamic hub directory of all calculators (English).
- [/fr/calculateurs/](https://www.simulateurplus.com/fr/calculateurs/) - Dynamic hub directory of all calculators (French).
